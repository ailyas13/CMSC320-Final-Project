1. Introduction

According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), about 1 in 6 people gets sick by consuming contaminate food, and these foodborne diseases cause 48 million illnesses and about 3,000 deaths each year in the United States. out source

Food safety is an important aspect to reduce the risk of diseases and deaths that are caused by consuming contaminated foods and beverages.

The Licensure & Regulatory Services Program inspects all the licensed retail food establishments in Montgomery County. Using LRSP's Food Inspection data, I will present and examine the frequency of the inspections conducted in the Montgomery County's restaurants on a routine basis.
